Auto reminders give periodic 'Stay in Touch' notifications for a contact.


How it works

1) Set a duration for 'Stay in touch' (for example 2 weeks)

2) Notification appears after 2 weeks.

3) Contact the person (via phone call, SMS, Whatsapp, Telegram or etc).

4) Vlix automatically records this activity (You can also record it manually in Vlix).

5) Notification appears again 2 weeks after this last activity.
