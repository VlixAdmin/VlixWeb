Send unlimited bulk SMS

In free version, a bulk SMS can only have 150 recipients. You can create as many bulk SMS you want but each bulk SMS can only have 150 recipients (You can watch ads to temporarily increase this limit). Upgrade to PRO for unlimited recipients.

No bulk SMS signature

In free version, all SMS will be appended with a message 'Sent Using Vlix'. Upgrade to PRO to remove this signature/watermark.

Unlimited contact groups

You contacts can be grouped for easy searching and sending targeted bulk SMS. In free version you can only manage 7 groups. Upgrade to PRO for unlimited groups.

Unlimited auto reminders

Auto reminders gives periodic notifications for a contact. This ensures you always stay in touch with a contact.

How it works

1) Set a duration for 'Stay in touch' (for example 2 weeks)

2) Notification appears after 2 weeks.

3) Contact the person (via phone call, SMS, Whatsapp, Telegram or etc).

4) Vlix automatically records this contact activity. You can also record this activity manually in Vlix.

5) Notification appears again 2 weeks after this last activity.

You can only have 7 auto reminders in the FREE version.
